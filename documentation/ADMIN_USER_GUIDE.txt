RAVENVOTE - SYSTEM DOCUMENTATION
USER ROLE: ADMINISTRATOR

1. YOUR ROLE IN THE SYSTEM
As a RavenVote Administrator, you are responsible for managing the integrity of the election process. Your portal allows you to manage voters, candidates, and monitor live results.

2. CORE WORKFLOWS
- Voter Management: Import student lists, verify registration status, and monitor who has voted.
- Candidate Management: Add candidates for different positions, upload their photos, and manage their slogans/manifestos.
- Election Monitoring: View real-time statistics of voter turnout and live results per position.
- System Security: Ensure only authorized students can access the voting screens using Index Number and OTP verification.

3. ETHICAL GUIDELINES
- Neutrality: Maintain strict impartiality throughout the election process.
- Privacy: Protect student data and ensure the secrecy of individual votes.
- Transparency: Provide accurate reports and logs if requested by the electoral commission.
